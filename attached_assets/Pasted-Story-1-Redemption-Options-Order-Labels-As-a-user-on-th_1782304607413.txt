Story 1 — Redemption Options Order & Labels
As a user on the Points tab,
 I want to see exactly 4 redemption options displayed in a specific order,
 So that the options are clear, correctly labelled, and logically sequenced.
Acceptance Criteria:
The 4 redemption options must appear in this exact order in the 2×2 grid (left to right, top to bottom):
Position 1 (top-left): Premium
Position 2 (top-right): HSA Contribution
Position 3 (bottom-left): Healthcare Savings Giftcards
Position 4 (bottom-right): Copay/Deductible Credit
No other redemption options appear. This is the complete and final list.
The label for each option uses the exact titles listed above — no rewording or abbreviation.

Story 2 — Healthcare Savings Giftcards Description Text
As a user viewing the redemption options on the Points tab,
 I want the Healthcare Savings Giftcards option to display a short description beneath its title,
 So that I understand what this option does before tapping it.
Acceptance Criteria:
The Healthcare Savings Giftcards card displays the following sub-text beneath its title, verbatim:
 "Add funds to your health savings accounts."
This description text appears on the card itself, not in a tooltip or popup.
